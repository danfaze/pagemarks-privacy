Privacy Policy for PageMarks

Effective Date: September 2025

PageMarks is a Chrome extension that helps users mark and navigate pages more efficiently. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

What Data We Collect

PageMarks does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. All markers, settings, and preferences are stored locally in your browser using Chrome storage APIs.

How Data Is Used

All information remains on your device and is only used to provide the extension’s core features (saving markers, themes, and preferences). Data is never sent to external servers.

Third Parties

PageMarks does not share information with third parties. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising cookies.

Your Choices

You can clear your saved markers and settings at any time from the extension’s settings panel.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@fusiontechnology.com
